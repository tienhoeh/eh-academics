Singapore International School Application Guide

Comprehensive guide to Singapore international school applications, requirements and timelines

中文版

School Types Overview

International Baccalaureate (IB) Schools

Offering IB programmes including Primary Years Programme (PYP), Middle Years Programme (MYP) and Diploma Programme (DP). Ideal for students planning overseas university education.

  • Globally recognized qualifications
  • Develops critical thinking skills
  • Multicultural environment

British Curriculum Schools

Following the UK National Curriculum with IGCSE and A-Level examinations. Perfect for students targeting UK universities.

  • Rigorous academic standards
  • Subject specialization depth
  • Direct pathway to UK universities

American Curriculum Schools

Providing US High School Diploma and Advanced Placement (AP) courses. Suitable for students applying to US universities.

  • Flexible course selection
  • Holistic development approach
  • US university preparation

Application Timeline

Jan - Mar

Spring Application Period

Most schools open applications for the following academic year. We recommend preparing all application materials in advance.

Apr - Jun

Interviews and Assessments

Schools conduct student interviews and academic assessments. Parent interviews are typically scheduled during this period.

Jul - Aug

Admission Results

Schools release admission decisions. Students must confirm their place and pay deposit within the specified timeframe.

Aug - Sep

New Academic Year Begins

Complete registration procedures, attend new student orientation, and officially start the new academic year.

Application Document Checklist

Student Documents

  • Passport copy
  • Birth certificate
  • Last 2 years' school reports
  • Teacher/Principal recommendation letters
  • Language proficiency certificates
  • Personal statement/Portfolio

Parent Documents

  • Parents' passport copies
  • Employment letter/Work permit
  • Proof of residence address
  • Vaccination records
  • Medical insurance proof
  • Application fee payment proof

Application Tips

Research Early

Thoroughly understand your target schools' educational philosophy, curriculum offerings, and campus culture to ensure alignment with your child's needs.

Attend Open Houses

Participate in school open days whenever possible to interact with teachers and current parents, gaining first-hand insights.

Plan Ahead

Start preparing applications at least one year in advance. Popular schools are highly competitive, so early application brings peace of mind.

Showcase Uniqueness

Highlight your child's unique talents and interests to make your application materials more compelling and persuasive.
